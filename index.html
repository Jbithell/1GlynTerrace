Self Catering Seaside Holiday Cottage in the Idyllic Village of Borth-y-Gest

Situated in the heart of the village of Borth-y-Gest, North Wales, the self-catering holiday property is 100 yards from the sea with the Lyn Peninsula's finest sandy beaches only 3 minutes walk away. The cottage is situated within 3 miles of the magnificent Snowdonia National Park, and the local town, Porthmadog, is home to the world famous Ffestiniog and Welsh Highland railways.
